About Kakashi

Kakashi Hatake is a Konohagakure Jonin, a former ANBU, and the leader of Team 7. He is the son of Sakumo Hatake (White Fang) and, like his father, he is regarded as a genius among his peers. When the fourth Great Ninja War End because of disability of Lady Tsunade Kakashi is the only candidate for 6th Hokage of Hidden Leaf. He is the youngest Jonin of all time in Konoha and as a student of Minato 4th Hokage

Leader of Legends

Kakashi assigned the Leader of Team 7 Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno by 3rd Hokage. This team would do wonders under Kakashi. Unfornately tie of team 7 broken when sauske leave Konoha and joins Orochimaru. However Kakashi still trains Naruto and Sakura.
